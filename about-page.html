MISSION STATEMENT

As I continue to learn and create I want to focus on building sustainable art practices through recycling and reusing commonly discarded items.

ABOUT THE ARTIST

Welcome! My name is Franny Haight and I'm a Chicago/Minneapolis based artist and designer. I am currently a senior studing at the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities, where I am majoring in Graphic Design with a Minor in Art.

I have a wide variety of interests that often overlap into multimedia work. Some of my favorite mediums are simple pen and paper, collage, block printing, eco printing, clay/ceramics, apparel design, and digital artwork!
